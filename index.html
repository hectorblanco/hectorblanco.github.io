about me

I was born in Yucatan, Mexico, I moved to Barcelona and I'm currently living in Berlin. I graduated in Multimedia. My goal is to find a job as a videogame designer.

Fascinated by design and illustration, both are a part of me. I am always drawing in my free time and that is something I've always loved doing. I consider myself pretty enthusiastic about learning the variety of colouring styles and enhancing my drawing techniques.

Here, you can know a little more about me and see the gallery. If you want to know more about my artwork, please visit my website on DeviantArt to see the full gallery.
