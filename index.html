about me

Fascinated by design and illustration, both are a part of me. I am constantly drawing in my free time and that is something I have always loved doing. I consider myself pretty enthusiastic about learning the variety of colouring styles and enhancing my drawing techniques.

I studied Communication Sciences in my hometown MÃ©rida, in Mexico. I lived in Barcelona for two years, where I worked in Online Marketing. Now I moved to Berlin looking forward to turn my hobby into my life's work as a videogame designer.

Besides drawing, I enjoy playing videogames, reading comic books and watching fantasy and science fiction movies. When possible I love to travel with my wife to meet other cultures, otherwise restaurants are my favourite place to discover new cultures through their gastronomy.
